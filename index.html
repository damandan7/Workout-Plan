SHREDDED - My Weekly Workout Plan

Monday

  • Warm-Up
  • Push-Ups (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Rows (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)
  • Leg Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 12-15 reps per arm)

Tuesday

  • Cardio (20 minutes)
  • Plank (3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds)
  • Russian Twists (3 sets of 12-15 reps per side)
  • Leg Raises (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Lunges (3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg)
  • Dumbbell Bench Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Wednesday

  • Pull-Ups (4 sets of 6-8 reps)
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Bent-Over Dumbbell Rows (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)
  • Push-Ups (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Bicep Hammer Curls (3 sets of 12-15 reps per arm)
  • Tricep Kickbacks (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)

Thursday

  • Cardio (20 minutes)
  • Plank (3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds)
  • Russian Twists (3 sets of 12-15 reps per side)
  • Leg Raises (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Lunges (3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg)

Friday

  • Cardio (30 minutes)
  • Cool Down (10 minutes)
  • Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Flyes (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Incline Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Lat Pulldowns (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Shrugs (3 sets of 12-15 reps per arm)
  • Tricep Dips (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
