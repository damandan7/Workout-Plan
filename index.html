SHREDDED - My Weekly Workout Plan

Monday (Chest Day)

  • Warm-Up
  • Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell Flyes (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Incline Bench Press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Chest Flyes Machine (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Tuesday (Arm Day)

  • Warm-Up
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Tricep Dips (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Hammer Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Skull Crushers (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Wednesday (Legs Day)

  • Warm-Up
  • Squats (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Lunges (3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg)
  • Leg Press (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Leg Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)

Thursday (Core Day)

  • Warm-Up
  • Plank (3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds)
  • Russian Twists (3 sets of 12-15 reps per side)
  • Leg Raises (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Bicycle Crunches (3 sets of 12-15 reps per side)

Friday (Arm Day)

  • Warm-Up
  • Push-Ups (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
  • Tricep Kickbacks (3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm)
  • Hammer Curls (3 sets of 10-12 reps)
